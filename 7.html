Friendship through the years

When we met, years
ago, we started to be friends.
at that point, I knew that
our friendship would never
end. the little disagreements
we sometimes would have
would turn, and
make us madder, especially
at other friends. our fights
never lasted long, and we never
had more than two.
but even with those few fights,
never once did our friendship
end. I know for a fact
that we'll be friends
for eternity.
