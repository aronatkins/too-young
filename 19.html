Untitled

Drifting apart, going
our own ways.
It seems like only
yesterday when we
became friends, even
though it was years ago.
We've gone through
a lot together, but
no one stays friends
forever. I'd say we were
fair weathered friends,
but it's not always true.
It seems like all
we ever do is fight,
and most of the fights
we don't know what
they are about.
Someday, after we
finally go our
separate ways,
our paths will
meet again, and like
Brandy's song,
we again will be,
Best Friends.
