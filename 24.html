Untitled

I look at you
through my tears,
watching you,
figuring out all your fears.
I know I can't stay away,
not even for just today.
I look so sad,
I want to cry,
but then you'll tell me
it's time to say bye-bye.
Bye-bye to my love for you.
To me, you will never be true.
I tell you I love you,
but you look the other way
though I whisper to you ‘stay.'
Again you see my tears
and choose to look away.
There I am,
crying for you to stay.
Are you scared-
I see it in your eyes,
but then you cover
it up with all of those lies.
You say I'm a friend,
but I promise you my feelings
for you will never end.
I need you.
I want you.
I kiss you.
I miss you.
I hold you.
I love you.
