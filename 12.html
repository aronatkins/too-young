Untitled

my bright eyes
shining my love
into your life.
but not receiving
any love in return.
my world is so
cold because of you.
you stole my love
the moment I first
laid eyes on you,
and to this very day,
you have not returned it,
nor have you loved me.
all I want is to be
loved and understood.
my life is so cold,
lonely, and painful.
all I need is love,
and someone to
understand me.
