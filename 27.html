Untitled

So close to time,
so close to sadness.
Tears roll down your face,
unknown, unnamed.
There's always one question
that stays in your heart
throughout the years;
Why?
There is no answer in time.
There is no answer in sadness.
But are there answers in your tears?
The tears that always seem
to say so much, yet always
seem to say so little.
Is the answer there,
in the tears, or is there
no answer to be found?
