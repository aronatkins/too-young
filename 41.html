Untitled

When we met,
we were so different,
not knowing we were
the same.
We have so much in
common that we
never realized
until that time when
we became friends.
Since then,
you've always
been there for me,
through thick and thin.
Whenever I had to
cry, you were the first
to lend me a shoulder to
cry on, a consoling arm,
and some calming
words. How we became
such good friends
from two different worlds,
is beyond just words.
It looks deep into
spirit and soul,
and the many things
we share. For one,
our taste in guys is the same.
We have differences,
though. You;
the outgoing type
who always has to
have the first and
last word. Compared
to you, I'm as
shy and quiet as
it gets (I'm working
on it).Yet I believe
that our differences
along with our similarities
bring us closer together
as friends, within a
friendship I hope
lasts forever.
