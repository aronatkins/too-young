Friendship throughout life

Knowing each other only
a little when we became
friends, who knew we ever
had so much in common.
the worst thing that we
had in common, brought on
many fights. my sister also
brought on many fights.
that one thing so horrible
to have in common is that
we're both very stubborn
and think we are always right.
but even with all those fights,
we stopped fighting, and
eventually we started again.
but one thing that makes
our friendship so different
from others, is when we fight,
we always are friends in the
end. I hope that no matter
what happens, we will stay
friends, always.
