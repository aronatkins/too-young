I Understand Tears

Though I'm young,
and don't always
understand what's
going on,
I understand tears.
Love and life are
still confusing
to me, but as
you sit there with
tears in your
eyes, I cry
with you because
I understand tears.
You are like a sister
to me and I hate
to see you cry
because
I understand tears.
