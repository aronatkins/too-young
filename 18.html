Saying Good-bye

Too quickly the
time has past
since we first met.
Too soon has the
time come to
say good-bye.
I learned a lot
from you that
I thought I knew.
Rejection and love
are two of the things
I have learned from you.
But even though the
time has come
to say good-bye,
it doesn't mean
forever. This may
be the end of the
road for some things,
but for my love and
our friendship,
the road will always
continue.
