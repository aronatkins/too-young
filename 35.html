Untitled

When she walked
upon the earth,
the flowers bloomed
around her.
But now, she walks above.
She took the flowers
with her, and they
no longer bloom below.
When you see a
rainbow in heaven,
you'll know why.
She brought those
without love, caring,
and honesty, and
opened her heart
to the world,
just to get stabbed,
and go through
more pain than
you can imagine.
This pain stays
with me today
as every other day.
I carry the
burden of life
and death as
the two meet
when the flower
tries to bloom
in both places
and wins.
Flower is love.
You know her
as Love.
I know her as
Mom.
