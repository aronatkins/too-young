Untitled

As I remember days
gone by,
I sit here with
you in my heart
and a tear in my eye. I wait for you
every day,
wishing when you were
here, you said you
would stay.
Day by day,
night by night,
I wait for
you to be by my side.
My feelings for you,
I can no longer hide.
My love grows stronger
every day;
hoping when you
return, you'll hear
what I have to say.
I don't want to
say good-bye,
but if I lose you,
I know I'll die.
My heart longs
for the sweet words
you whisper to me
when we are together,
which I wish was
forever.
But time will tell,
and hearts will love.
Everyone asks me why
I wait for you and
I tell them because;
I love you,
and I can't wait until
I have you.
