Untitled

In that distant galaxy
where two hearts
become one,
where people talk
of lovers' love
until hearts have won.
Where the shooting
stars begin to
shine throughout
the pale blue night.
And searching
in that galaxy
for true love's
only light,
we stand together-
yet apart; afraid
of loving love.
Within all the galaxies,
there is only one time
to find that
special love.
I found only you
within my heart;
I'll search
no more, yet
wait until you
aren't afraid.
Find your love
for me and pick
it up from
whatever galaxy
it was laid.
I'll wait for you
until death does
come and galaxies
fall upon;
that one chance
of hope dissolves
into a lonely
loss: and
when hope is
through, love it
will toss.
Within the
chance; either
love me, or
don't give me
a second glance.
Release me and let me be free.
