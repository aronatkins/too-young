My Brother

As long as I can remember;
since the day I was born,
his loving arms were waiting
to catch me when I fall.
He's always there to
help me and guide me
along the way,
and I can always
call him, no matter
what I have to say.
I tell him that I love him
from the bottom of my
heart, and he makes
me proud. No matter
how fat away he travels,
I know he's always
there. No matter the
arguments we get in,
I know he'll always care. I
always try to be there
for him, like he is for me,
though he's on his own.
When I think of
the times when we
were young, and just
sat in his room together;
just thinking of
the past makes
me smile and
then I can't wait
to see him. He moves
on with his life,
though I still wish
the days were still
when we were young.
But he goes off and
builds his life.
And still, to this
very day, his loving
arms are there,
to help me along
the way. Now
brother, I say
to you, as long as
you are happy in
your life you
built since you
left home, that's
all that matters.
Just remember,
I'll always
be your sister and
I'll always be
here for you. But
most important,
remember; no
matter what,
I'll always love you.
