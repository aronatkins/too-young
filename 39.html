A Good-bye

When it's time
to say good-bye,
till the death,
I will not cry.
After tears
comes the grief and sorrow;
Knowing that you
won't be there tomorrow.
I say good-bye
as the tears fall;
knowing you'll
never be there when I call.
Your name will
always be in my mind;
I knew you to be
so gentle and kind.
But the time has
finally come to say;
good-bye to today,
tomorrow, and yesterday.
Though it's sad,
I know it's for the best;
so you don't suffer more pain,
you'll be put to your rest.
