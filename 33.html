Mama's Arms

Mama's arms have
always been there
to help me when
I fall.
To tell me right
from wrong
and about life
and all.
My mama's arms
have always been
there to guide
me on my way,
as far back as
I can remember,
until yesterday.
Yesterday, mama's arms
were gone, to never
return.
As you sadly watch
them take her
away,
you cry as
you remember
yesterday.
You say good-bye
to those loving arms
that held you
until the tears
were gone.
Good-bye mama.
