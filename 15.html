Untitled

in this
time and place,
life doesn't matter.
people don't care.
they only watch out
for themselves,
and don't give a damn
about others.
but if you step back,
for once, from being
selfish, you will
see the truth in the
world around you.
you will realize that
life isn't always as you
want it.
people don't realize
how much they have,
and take everything
for granted,
and when they lose
something or someone
they love,
their life turns so
dark and hateful.
then they turn to
everyone to have pity
one them, and to love them,
but because they don't
understand, they
will never be loved,
nor allow themselves to love,
because people just don't care.
