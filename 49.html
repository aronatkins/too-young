Untitled

Your smile is so heavenly.
As I look into your eyes,
I forget everything else around me
but you.
My feelings for you
are so strong, though you'll
never know.
Time passes by, but I'll never tell you
how I feel.
As we walk by each other,
we smile and say hi,
but exchange no other
words.
I long to tell you how
I feel, but I'm afraid
you don't feel the same way.
I don't want you to break
my heart,
because my tears
will never stop
falling down my face.
And your smile,
will ever again
look so heavenly.
You are someone I could
love forever,
but I don't want
to chance my heart,
because what if you
don't feel the same way
about me,
as I feel about you?
These questions run
through my mind,
day after day.
Will my questions
ever be answered?
Will my dreams
of having you
ever be fulfilled?
I wait for the
day questions
will be answered,
and dreams will
come true.
You are always on my mind,
always in my heart.
But until the day you know
of my love, we
will never have
the chance of being together,
unless you love me
but don't know how to show it,
or you are afraid, as I am,
that your heart
will be broken with
no one to pick up the pieces,
but I'll always love you.
If you need someone,
I'll always be there for you.
And if someone
breaks your heart,
I'll always be there to pick up the pieces.
