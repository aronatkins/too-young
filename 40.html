Best Friend

You, sitting all alone
with tears in your
gray eyes.
Listen closely because
what I have to say
is hard to say,
but important.
You've been there
for me through
everything. Despite
all of my tears, my anger,
and my fears, you have
been there to listen.
I love you, but not
just as a friend.
Mike, I love you
more than words
can say,
but my love isn't just
for today. My love is
undying, strong until
the end. My love is
like a rainbow, it will
be there after every
storm. My love is like the
sun; it shines bright
to show me the path
I want to take.
This is the first time
I have told you
of my love. The
love that started
so long ago and that
will never end.
Just because I have
told you my true
feelings, there is
no need to run
away, frightened of love.
I hope that we can
be friends and let
nothing come between up
because I don't
want to be without
a friend like you;
so sweet, caring, and
understanding.
Just remember;
I'll always
love you.
