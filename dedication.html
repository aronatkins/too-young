Knowing Everything Too Young: Dedication

I dedicate this book to Joshua Bowling, my first true love and the one who inspired me to write most of my poems, one of my best friends, Christine Perkins, who has always been there for me, my friend Vinny-his name says it all, and my loving mother Linda Atkins, who died when I was 11, in 1993. She inspired a lit of my poems. I also want to dedicate this to all my friends from U-Mass for a great summer and wonderful friendships to be cherished forever. And of course, I can't forget all of the rest of my friends who have always been there for me. Last, but not least, I want to dedicate this to Erika and Krystal and all of the other foster sisters and brothers I have had. I know you're out there somewhere and I want you to know I love you and think of you often.
Published by Aron C. Atkins as a gift for the author, December 1998.
