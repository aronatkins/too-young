Untitled

our friendship was

different from others.
we were like sisters,
except we didn't fight
as much, but when we
did, the fights were over
the stupidest things.
but in the end, we
always were friends.
every time I had a
problem, you were always
there for me. every time
you had a problem,
I was always there for you.
at one point, you were
the only one
I could turn to.
the only one I trusted.
now, our friendship is
still different from others,
because our friendship
isn't there.
even though I wish it was.
