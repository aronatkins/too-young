Friends

You want to
be friends,
you say, who wants
to be lovers?
then smile and
walk away, leaving me
to cry my tears.
leaving me staring
after you, yelling,
swearing after you
to come back to me.
you never even gave
me one chance, yet
you walk away.
you want to
be friends.
I want to be lovers.
