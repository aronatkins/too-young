Untitled

Leaving all of
My friends behind
Was the hardest thing
I've ever had to do.
The tears never
Stop rolling down
My face.
They don't realize my
Tears because I turn
Away so they don't see
How much I miss them.
Every time I see them now,
I start to cry,
Wishing I never had to
Leave them.
I turn my head
So they don't
See my pain,
And I cry.
