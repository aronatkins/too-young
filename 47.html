Untitled

Two different worlds,
meeting to become one.
But we're so far apart,
there is no chance
to meet, no time for
love. Words spoken
though not heart; that
is the only way I know
you. But I've seen you.
You've never seen me, but no matter
how many miles between us,
and the way we never knew
each other besides those few
words, you have become
someone special to me,
someone I can never give up.
You seem to be different
that anyone I know, but
maybe that's why I love
you so much. But no one,
not even you, will ever
know how I feel.
