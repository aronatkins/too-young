Life

From her womb I came
never knowing why
into this life of shame
so cold lonely and dry

A world full laughter and tears
people walking in darkness
in a place sad and heartless

And from above we came
and now we shall return
away from this shame
my love for her will still burn

Too soon she left me
gone forever
I will meet up with her
at the end of never
