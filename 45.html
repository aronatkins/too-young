Colors

Would you love me
If my hair was a different color,
My skin a different shade?
I can see you don't love me now,
But colors seem to make a difference.

Would you love me if
The sky was black,
And the earth white?
Would you love me if all
You saw was red?

Could you love me if
The colors of the world
Were what you wanted them to be
Instead of what they are?

Would there be love between us
If I were different colors,
Colors that seem to make
Such a big difference in your life?

You walk away,
As the colors fly with
The wind and
Color the earth.

You tell me there's no
Going back,
And the colors won't be
That of love.,
But that of friendship.

Do colors make you blind
To the world around you?
Do they shut your eyes so that
You can't see the colors of love?

Would the colors change
How you see me,
And show you to love me?

Do you still follow the colors,
Go wherever they take you,
To the worlds beyond,
With your colors of life
Exactly how you want them?

Would you love me
If my hair was a different color,
My skin a different shade?
I can see you don't love me now,
But colors seem to make a difference.
