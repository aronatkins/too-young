The Shadow of Darkness

I walk in the
shadow of darkness,
and I cry alone.
The path I must take
is no longer clear to me,
ever since you left me.
The days have become long
and the hours endless,
ever since you were gone.
I wait in the
shadow of darkness,
and I cry alone.
The reasons of life
have faded before me,
ever since you walked
out of my life.
The tears roll down my face,
and I can't tell you
why they're falling.
I love you,
but you are gone.
I walk in the
shadow of darkness,
and I cry alone.
When you were here,
I was never alone;
you were always
by my side.
Every mistake I made,
you were there to comfort me,
and tell me everything
will be all right.
Whenever I cried,
you were there
to lend me a shoulder
to cry on,
and caring arms,
holding me until
I stopped crying.
You would whisper
consoling words
as I cried over the phone
late at night,
but now it doesn't matter.
You are gone.
I walk in the
shadow of darkness,
and I cry alone.
