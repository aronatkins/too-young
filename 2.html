Untitled

I need someone to wrap
their arms around me.
I am sad, so sad.
and I cry out
for those loving arms.
but no one hears me
no one hears my cries.
so I sit swimming
in my tears
I cradle myself with my hurt.
I know for a moment,
I am all alone.
now I pray that the moment
won't last for long.
but it lasts forever
and I drown in my tears.
