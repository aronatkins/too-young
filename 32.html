Untitled

At age nineteen,
he sits and cries
when he thinks
no one is around.
He bears his soul
to the world.
And still today,
at age twenty-two,
he sits and cries
some more, never
knowing anyone
knows about
his anger, about
his tears.
Thinking no one
knows about
his pain or
about his
fears.
