Tears from Heaven

I said good-bye to you
that night so long ago.
I never knew it would be
forever an eternity.
If I had, I would have
held you close, never
to let you go.
Now as the rain
falls and wipes
away my tears,
I know you are
crying too.
I know those tears
from heaven mean
love, honesty, and tears.
I let those tears
fall down from heaven,
onto my soul.
Tears from heaven.
Pray for you,
and on that stormy night
you walked outside.
Never to return.
Tears in Heaven.
I miss you so much.
Tears in Heaven.
I love you more than
words can say.
Tears in Heaven.
Time to say good-bye.
Tears in Heaven.
