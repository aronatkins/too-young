Mindy

You're always there,
and you always
seem to care.
You're one I could
never forget,
being such
a great friend to me.
I'll keep you
close to my heart
and always in my mind.
You are one of the
few people that can make
me smile, and I know
I can always count
on you and I hope
you know you
can always count on
me to be there when
you need a friend, or
just need someone to listen,
or a shoulder to cry on.
Love you!
